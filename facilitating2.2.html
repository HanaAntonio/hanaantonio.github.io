Emergency Neurological Life Support Training

Universitas Indonesia Hospital


• As a pioneer of this training, I managed to facilitate and coordinate a team to host Emergency Neurological Life Support Training at Universitas Indonesia Hospital. The team consisted of medical practitioner, nurses, and pharmacists from the Emergency Department.

• We invited participants consisting of general practitioners, resident doctors, and neurologists from all over Indonesia. Through this training, not only learn about theoretical and hands-on workshops, but participants also had the chance to engage with fellow medical practitioners.

• Thus, inter-collaboration between healthcare workers is induced. After this training, participants are wished to have adequate neurological emergency skills that can be applied at their workplaces. In the future, we wish to hold this event annually.
