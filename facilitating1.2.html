Health Talk with Parkway Cancer Centre

GWS Medika (Member of Sinar Mas Group)


• Facilitated an engaging health talk in collaboration with Parkway Cancer Centre (PCC), focusing on lymphoma, myeloma, and hematopoietic stem cell transplants. The main speakers were oncologists from PCC in Singapore.

• The discussion was lively and interactive, with participants posing insightful questions about blood cancers, lymphatic cancers, and myeloma. Topics discussed also include understanding blood and lymphatic cancers and treatment approaches for patients suspected of having these cancers in a clinic setting.

• Overall, the event was a great success, fostering a deeper understanding of these complex conditions and their treatments.
